Our Story

From our humble beginnings to building a nationwide logistics network.

A Vision for Better Logistics

Areti Alliance was founded in 2019 by logistics experts who recognized a critical gap in the last-mile delivery market. With decades of combined experience in transportation and supply chain management, our founders set out to create a delivery service that would truly meet the needs of modern businesses.

Our name "Areti" comes from the Greek word for "excellence" and "virtue" – qualities that have guided our business from day one. We started with a small fleet of vehicles serving the Atlanta area, and quickly expanded to become a trusted nationwide logistics partner for businesses of all sizes.

Our Growth Journey
